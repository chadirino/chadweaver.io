What it does

Argument Evaluator is a structured debate and logic tool. You start with an assertion — any claim you want to examine — then build a case by adding arguments for and against it.

The tool uses AI to evaluate each argument: checking for logical fallacies, identifying weak reasoning, and surfacing what's actually being assumed. The goal is to make you a sharper, more honest thinker.

Why I built it

Good reasoning is rare. Most arguments online are a mix of bad logic, emotional appeals, and tribal signaling — and it's genuinely hard to untangle. I wanted a tool that holds your reasoning accountable in real time, the way a good debate coach would.

This connects directly to my interest in epistemic infrastructure — building tools that help people reason better, not just feel more confident.

Planned features

01
Claim builder — add and organize claims for or against any assertion with a clean, structured interface.
02
AI fallacy detection — automatically flags common logical errors like straw man, ad hominem, false dichotomy, and more.
03
Argument scoring — rates the strength and coherence of the overall case on both sides.
04
Share & compare — share your argument map with others and see how they evaluate the same question.
